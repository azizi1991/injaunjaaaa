About Us

InjaOnja

InjaOnja is a startup that provide platform for immigrants and travellers to connect with hosts from their homelandoffering culturally familiar accommodations and vehicle rentals and finding fellow travellers and tour organizers. Our initial focus is on the Iranian community abroad, leveragin our familiarity with Iranian culture to ensure comfort and trust.

Service Description

InjaOnja connects immigrants and travellers with hosts from their homeland, offering culturally familiar short-term accommodations and vehicle rentals.Users can also find travel partners to share their journeys and split expenses, making the experience more enjoyable and cost-effective. Additionally, the app allows users to organize tourist tours.Each version of the app is available in the local language, ensuring ease of use.
